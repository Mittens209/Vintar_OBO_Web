APPLICATION PROCESS

1

Prepare Documents

Gather all required documents from the Downloads section.

  • Completed application form
  • Notarized affidavits
  • Property documents
2

Submit Application

Visit the Municipal Building Official's Office with your complete requirements:

Address: Municipal Hall, Vintar, Ilocos Norte

Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

3

Payment & Processing

After document verification:

  • Pay the required fees at the Treasurer's Office
  • Receive your official receipt
  • Processing time: 3-5 working days
4

Claim Permit

Return with your claim stub to receive:

  • Approved Building Permit
  • FSEC Certificate
  • Official stamped plans

Important Notes:

  • Incomplete applications will not be accepted
  • Bring valid ID when claiming your permit
  • Construction cannot begin until permit is issued

DOCUMENTARY REQUIREMENTS FOR BUILDING PERMIT APPLICATION

(Single Dwelling Residential / Commercial / Industrial / Others)

Special Requirements:

  • Electrical Analysis (for 100 Amperes MAIN and above)
  • Soil Test (for 3-Storey & up) - 1 copy
  • Application for Locational Clearance - Notarized
  • Authorization Letter - 4 copies

Required Plans (to be signed and sealed by Licensed Professionals):

A) Architectural Documents
B) Civil/Structural Documents
C) Electrical Documents
D) Mechanical Documents
E) Sanitary Documents
F) Plumbing Documents
G) Electronics Documents
H) Geodetic Documents
I) Fire Protection Plan (If applicable)

Notes:

  • All the fully accomplished forms and requirements must be fastened in a LONG FOLDER except for the Drawing Plans and reports
  • ONLY A COMPLETE AND COMPLIANT APPLICATION WILL BE ACCEPTED
